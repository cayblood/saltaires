Guest Nights October 19 and 26

Do you love to sing? Are you, or a guy you know, interested in singing in a men's barbershop chorus at a competitive level, and sing with the artistry and harmonies you hear from the Saltaires?

Then come to Guest Night! Every rehearsal night in October (the 19th and 26th) is a chance for you to learn new songs, sing a tag, catch up with old friends, make new ones, enjoy some snacks, and sing some songs. It's our favorite time of the year!

We have posted sheet music and learning tracks to 'When I Lift Up My Head', 'Tonight', and 'Put Your Head on My Shoulder' for all to access - only to be used for this event!

Please share this link with those who you also think would express interest in coming! Pick your favorite part and come prepared to sing with us on the risers one or both weeks! The more, the merrier - we plan on having full risers!

7pm at the Garbett Center - 700 N 200 W, Salt Lake City, UT 84103
