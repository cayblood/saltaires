Director Brady Allred

The Saltaires are delighted to announce Dr. Brady R. Allred as our new musical director. Dr. Allred has more than three decades of experience as a professional conductor. He was a professor of music and director of choral conducting at the University of Utah until 2010. He is currently the artistic director and conductor of the Salt Lake Choral Artists, where he has directed multiple award-winning choirs since 2004.

During his years with SLCA, Dr. Allred has led his choirs to several 1st place finishes in choral festivals and competitions around the world.

He has served on international juries for competitions in Austria, Belgium, France, Germany, Ireland, Slovenia, Spain, Brazil, Indonesia, Japan, China, Korea, Malaysia, and Thailand and has conducted over one hundred regional and All-State Choirs.

Dr. Allred earned a Bachelor of Music degree in theory/composition and flute performance from BYU (1985), a Master of Music degree in choral conducting from the Eastman School of Music (1987) and a Doctor of Musical Arts degree in conducting from Eastman (1990).

Brady sang with a barbershop quartet in college, and he is excited for his first experience directing a men's barbershop chorus. The Saltaires are looking forward to benefitting from his musical expertise and competitive spirit.
